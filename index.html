Pixeled Brewing Database

We developed a database for the Pixeled Brewing Website for our final project. Pixeled Brewing was founded by John-Paul Klein with the unique idea to fuse brewing with old, classic arcade games. A former group member, we decided to stick to his goal and finish the project that he started to renovate the Pixeled Brewing website.
